WHAT IF AMERICA
PLAYED THE OTHER FOOTBALL?

Suppose America's leagues never existed — no NBA, NFL, NHL, MLB, none of it — and every generational American athlete grew up in a soccer academy instead. Would Tyreek Hill torch Premier League fullbacks? Would Steph Curry's handles translate to Messi-grade dribbling? Is LeBron the greatest goalkeeper who never was? Is Simone Biles physically possible to tackle? This engine translates their measured athletic traits into soccer ratings, finds their position, and simulates the hypothetical USA XI against the world's best — including the cap-tied stars who'd line up against us.
